### AI-Powered Learning Platform for Children with Dyslexia

Drago is a bilingual (Arabic/English) educational platform designed to support Arabic-speaking children with dyslexia through personalized learning, artificial intelligence, and interactive educational experiences.

Our mission is to make learning more accessible, engaging, and adaptive while addressing real reading and phonological challenges.

---

## About Drago

Children with dyslexia in the Arab world still face limited access to specialized educational technology.

Drago was developed as a graduation project to bridge this gap by combining:

* Educational methodologies
* AI-powered assessment
* Accessibility-first design
* Game-based learning
* Personalized learning experiences

---

## Platform Features

### Educational Games

Drago includes four educational games designed to target different dyslexia-related challenges:

* **Volcano Words** — Speech recognition and pronunciation exercises
* **Reading Quest** — Reading comprehension activities
* **Word Hut** — Missing letters and spelling practice
* **Tomb Puzzle** — Sentence and word ordering

### AI & Personalization

* Personalized learning recommendations
* Adaptive learning paths
* Speech-based educational assessment
* Reading and pronunciation error analysis
* NLP-supported educational processing

### Doctor Dashboard

* Student progress tracking
* Session and assessment management
* Real-time monitoring and analytics

---

## Accessibility & Learning Experience

Drago was designed with accessibility at its core:

* Full Arabic RTL support
* OpenDyslexic readable font
* Child-centered UI experience
* Custom Drago mascot and visual identity
* Progressive difficulty levels
* Offline support through PWA

---

## Behind The Project

Throughout the journey, we:

* Studied dyslexia intervention approaches
* Designed a structured learning guide
* Built tailored datasets for educational activities
* Adapted Whisper-based speech recognition for reading exercises
* Applied NLP techniques for intelligent educational feedback
* Discussed requirements and validated ideas with specialists
* Prepared a research paper based on the project
